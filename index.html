The word data is actually the plural of datum; datum refers to a singel value, while data refers to a collection of values. Data can be analyzed with graphical techniques or numeral measures. I belive graphs are a visual represntation of data sets, making it easier to see patterns. Histograms, line graphs, pie charts and scatter plots are different visualisation in which data can be viewed. Data provides useful information in many areas of business applications.I have worked with many different software programs both proprietary and open source databases. The most important information in all databases is the DATA!

Working with various reporting tools has kept me up to date with what is occurring in the backend of these software databases and that has given me the ability to acquire, manipulate, and report on data. I enjoy knowing the business fundamentals, so that the data in which I am reporting has important business decisions. My curiosity allows me to dig further into resolving and exploring additional resources to improve reporting and make over all business decisions.
